Burrito

A delicious bean and cheese burrito that will make your mouth water.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 1 Can of refried pinto beans
  • 1 Bag of shredded cheese
  • 1 Bottle of hot sauce

Steps

  1. Heat refried pinto beans in pot over medium heat until simmering
  2. Heat tortilla in pan over medium heat until tortilla is hot and pliable.
  3. Place tortilla on plate and put beans into burrito.
  4. Add cheese and hot sauce to burrito and roll.
