Grilled Cheese

A delicious grilled cheese!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of bread
  2. Place buttered side on pan and sprinkle cheese on top
  3. Place piece of bread on top of cheese
  4. Flip grilled cheese after 3 minutes
  5. After 3 minutes, remove from pan and serve
